About

Limerick Institute of Technology, is a higher education which set across in Limerick and branch off in Thurles and Clonmel. LIT offers courses at level at six up to level 10. The main campus is based in Moylish park. , with two city centre campuses in Clare Street and Georges Quay in Limerick city. The LIT Ennis Learning Centre is based in the museum in Ennis town centre. LIT was awarded the Sunday Times Institute of Technology of the Year in 2008 and 2013 by the Sunday Times University Guide. The guide is the definitive guide to higher education in Ireland and the UK.
